OUR LEADERSHIP TEAM

Experience. Vision. Excellence.

Leading with Vision and Expertise

Our leadership team brings together decades of combined experience in construction, engineering, and business management. Each member of our board is committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity, innovation, and client service. Their diverse backgrounds and complementary skills enable Al-Mulhem to navigate complex challenges and seize emerging opportunities in the construction industry.

Under their guidance, we continue to expand our capabilities, embrace new technologies, and maintain our reputation as a trusted partner for projects of all scales and complexities.

Board of Directors

Abdullah Al-Mulhem

Chairman & Chief Executive Officer

With over 30 years of experience in the construction industry, Abdullah Al-Mulhem founded the company with a vision to bring world-class construction solutions to the region. His strategic leadership and commitment to quality have been instrumental in establishing Al-Mulhem as a leading contractor. He holds a degree in Civil Engineering and has led numerous landmark projects throughout his career.

Mohammed Al-Rashid

Managing Director

Mohammed brings 25 years of construction management expertise to Al-Mulhem. He oversees all operational aspects of the company, ensuring seamless project execution and client satisfaction. His background in structural engineering and project management has been crucial in developing our systematic approach to complex projects.

Eng. Khalid Al-Zahrani

Technical Director

As Technical Director, Khalid leads our engineering team and drives innovation in construction methodologies. With a Master's degree in Structural Engineering and professional certifications in advanced construction technologies, he ensures that Al-Mulhem stays at the forefront of industry developments.

Fahad Al-Otaibi

Chief Financial Officer

Fahad oversees all financial operations, strategic planning, and business development initiatives. His financial acumen and strategic thinking have helped Al-Mulhem maintain financial stability while pursuing growth opportunities.

Eng. Saud Al-Ghamdi

Director of Operations

Saud manages our day-to-day operations, coordinating between various divisions to ensure efficient resource allocation and timely project delivery. His background in industrial engineering and lean construction methodologies has helped optimize our processes.

Eng. Nasser Al-Dosari

Director of Quality & Safety

Nasser is responsible for maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety across all our projects. His comprehensive approach to quality management and safety protocols has earned Al-Mulhem industry recognition for excellence.

Our Leadership Philosophy

Servant Leadership

Our leaders are committed to serving our team members, clients, and communities. We believe that great leadership starts with listening, understanding, and empowering others to achieve their full potential.

Continuous Learning

We foster a culture of continuous learning and professional development. Our leadership team actively pursues knowledge of emerging technologies, industry best practices, and innovative management approaches.

Ethical Standards

Integrity and ethical conduct are non-negotiable. We hold ourselves and our team to the highest standards of professional ethics, transparency, and accountability in all business dealings.

Collaborative Approach

We believe in the power of collaboration. Our leadership team works closely with employees, clients, partners, and stakeholders to achieve shared goals and create lasting value.
