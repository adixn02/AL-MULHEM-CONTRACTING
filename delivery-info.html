Delivery & Info

How we deliver projects and what you need to know when working with Al-Mulhem Contracting.

Our Approach to Delivery

Al-Mulhem Contracting is committed to delivering projects on time and to the highest standards. We combine efficient solutions, sustainable practices, and local expertise to meet client requirements across building envelope, GFRC facades, waterproofing, precast composites, steel fabrication, fit-out, and landscaping.

Service Areas

We operate primarily in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with our head office in Jeddah (Al-Rehab Dist, St 9054). We serve clients across commercial, residential, hospitality, and industrial sectors and are well positioned to support projects in the wider Gulf region.

Project Process

From initial enquiry to handover, we work closely with clients and design teams. Our process typically includes: consultation and scope definition, detailed quotation and contract, programme planning, execution with regular updates, quality checks, and formal handover with defect liability support where agreed.
