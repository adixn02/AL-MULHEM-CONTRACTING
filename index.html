Al-Mulhem - Building Trust, One Project at a Time.

Al-Mulhem Contracting Company is one of the leading Building Envelope and GFRC Facade construction contractors specialized in Fiber-Reinforced Architectural Composite Manufacturing & Waterproofing Systems Application in the region. Since its foundation in 2002, it has played a key and influential role in many major iconic infrastructure projects.

We are a multi-national entity, ISO certified Company, multi-disciplined and specialized in diversified fields in construction services with over 1000 employees.

Responding to a growing concern for construction protection and building materials environmental problems, Al-Mulhem's product and service range has been selected by key clients in the construction industry to serve today's increasing demand for advanced, reliable and value engineered solutions.

Our staff includes architects, engineers, construction and business professionals who share a strong commitment to improving the sustainability of business operations and the built environment. Our capabilities are further enhanced through our regional and International network of subject matter experts whom we continuously work with to ensure a continuous growth in our R&D efforts to meet our clients' most demanding sustainability challenges.
